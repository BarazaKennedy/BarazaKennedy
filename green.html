Motto

Empowering community

Vision

To be a leading Force in cultivating a greener , healthier planet where communities thrive in harmony with nature, ensuring the preservation of Africa and the world's ecological heritage for the generations to come

Mission

To champion sustainable environmental practices,foster biodiversity conservation and empower communities to protect and preserve the natural treasures of Africa and the globe at large.

Thematic Area

The Green Nature Foundation focuses on climate action, afforestation, wildlife conservation, sustainable agriculture, clean water access, renewable energy, waste management, and recycling. It promotes environmental awareness, green technology, and eco-friendly practices to protect and restore nature for future generations.

sustainable development goals ▶

NGAF, in its selection of thematic areas, has also taken into consideration SDGs No 2, 3, 4, 6, 7, 11, 12, 13, 15, and 17, contributing directly to the following:

SDG 13: Climate Action

  • Focus on mitigating climate change through afforestation, clean energy initiatives, and sustainable practices.

SDG 15: Life on Land

  • Protect terrestrial ecosystems, combat deforestation, and promote biodiversity conservation.

SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

  • Promote renewable energy adoption and sustainable energy solutions in communities.

SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

  • Advocate for water conservation, clean water access, and improved sanitation practices.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

  • Encourage sustainable waste management, recycling, and eco-friendly practices.

SDG 4: Quality Education

  • Provide environmental education, eco-literacy, and training in sustainable practices.

SDG 2: Zero Hunger

  • Support sustainable agriculture and promote food security through innovative farming techniques.

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

  • Address environmental factors affecting health, such as pollution and unsafe sanitation.

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

  • Promote green urban planning, eco-friendly infrastructure, and sustainable city initiatives.

SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

  • Foster collaborations with governments, institutions, and other stakeholders to amplify impact.
